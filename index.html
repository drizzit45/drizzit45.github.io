A Grab ride can cost S$20 just to travel a few streets — and that's before the grooming even starts. Our transport fee covers the whole trip to your door, and your pet never has to leave home.

🏥 Vet-backed

Grooming salons are specifically listed by veterinary authorities as high-risk environments for kennel cough and respiratory infections. Cornell University's College of Veterinary Medicine names grooming salons alongside boarding kennels as places that put dogs at risk. The American Kennel Club's Chief Veterinary Officer similarly flags grooming shops as high-risk for disease exposure. With mobile grooming, your pet stays at home — away from unknown dogs and shared spaces entirely.

Sources: Cornell University — Risks of Kennel Cough · AKC — Kennel Cough in Dogs · AVMA — Canine Respiratory Disease
